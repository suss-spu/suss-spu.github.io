Hack the next chapter of learning.

The SUSS Future Learning Hackathon is a campus-based innovation sprint organised by Singapore University of Social Sciences, Singapore's university for lifelong learning. It brings together students, educators, builders, and industry partners to prototype practical ideas for learning, teaching, education use cases, and agentic AI.

This is not a generic coding event. The challenge focus is clear: build tools, workflows, and experiences that make education more human, more effective, and more scalable for the future of teaching and lifelong learning, in line with SUSS' emphasis on purposeful continuous learning, applied research, and impactful partnerships.

Challenge Themes

Proposed tracks for learning, teaching, and agentic AI.

These themes are designed to attract strong student teams while keeping the work anchored to real education needs. Each track can be supported by sponsoring partners through problem statements, APIs, datasets, mentors, judges, or pilot pathways.

01
AI for Teaching Design

Lesson planning, rubric generation, activity design, feedback support, and tools that help educators teach with more clarity and less repetitive workload.

02
Agentic AI for Student Support

Goal-setting agents, study coaches, onboarding assistants, advising workflows, and support systems that guide students across the learning journey.

03
Responsible AI in Assessment

Formative feedback, reflective learning, authentic assessment design, academic integrity support, and transparent AI-assisted evaluation experiences.

04
Education Use Cases for Lifelong Learning

Skills mapping, modular learning, workplace learning pathways, adult learner support, and systems that connect education to employability and growth.

05
Inclusive and Accessible Learning

Multilingual learning experiences, accessibility-first design, assistive technology, neuroinclusive tools, and equitable digital learning support.

06
Learning Analytics and Early Intervention

Insight dashboards, risk detection, timely nudges, and decision support that help educators identify opportunities to improve engagement and outcomes.

Benefits
